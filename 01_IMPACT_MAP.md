# BETTERMAN — 01: The 5-year impact map
**The bet (ratified 2026-06-11):** A storytelling engine for the unseen of Los Angeles.
Mike + young creatives he trains document the people LA looks past — homeless, disconnected, kids without a path — through photography, film, and realtime/AI media. Direct service is the act of seeing someone and telling their story right. By June 2031 the engine runs without him.

**Design constraints (from intake, never violate):**
- 2 hr/wk floor, 10 hr/wk ceiling — feast-or-famine; milestones must survive dead months
- <$1k/yr cash. Skills, gear, and network are the currency
- Never costs: son's baseball, health routine
- No charity-middleman structures. Radical transparency. Money goes to work, not institution
- Mike stays invisible; subjects and mentees are the stars
- Kids join where natural

## Phase 1 — Embed (Oct 2026 – Dec 2027)
Goal: learn the terrain from inside; earn trust; build the first body of work.
- [ ] Volunteering regularly with 1–2 chosen orgs (photography/documentation as the service)
- [ ] 12+ stories told (≈1/month pace, burst-tolerant)
- [ ] First publication channel live (site/insta/zine — invisible-author model)
- [ ] First measurable "minds" data: reach, responses, shares, org-reported outcomes
- [ ] Recruit mentee candidates from schools/league/industry networks (target: 3 by end 2027)
- Exit criteria: knows the space, trusted by orgs and subjects, has proof the stories move people

## Phase 2 — First cohort (2028)
Goal: stop being the only storyteller.
- [ ] First workshop cohort: 4–8 young creatives (school/league pipeline)
- [ ] Curriculum v1: ethics/consent first, then craft (photo, edit, story, AI tools)
- [ ] Cohort produces its own exhibited/published work — 25+ cumulative stories
- [ ] One public showing (gallery night, school event, projection — event-world network activated)
- Exit criteria: someone Mike trained tells a story that moves people, without Mike behind the camera

## Phase 3 — Engine (2029–2030)
Goal: the thing becomes self-propelling.
- [ ] 2nd + 3rd cohorts; first-cohort grads co-teach (engine coefficient > 0)
- [ ] 75+ cumulative stories; recurring venue/channel partnerships
- [ ] Decide structure: stay informal vs. fiscal sponsorship vs. own entity (revisit trust rule; only formalize if it serves the work)
- [ ] Render farm / AI pipeline put to work if useful: archive, immersive exhibits (Unreal/Notch), local-model tooling for mentees
- Exit criteria: cohorts run on a calendar that doesn't depend on Mike's free weeks

## Phase 4 — Handoff (2030 – Jun 2031)
Goal: prove it runs without him.
- [ ] Named successor(s) leading curriculum and cohorts
- [ ] Mike steps back to mentor-of-mentors + occasional shooter
- [ ] 150+ cumulative stories; documented reach in the hundreds of thousands
- [ ] The 2031 sentence is true: "Built a storytelling engine that changes how LA sees its unseen — and it runs without me."
